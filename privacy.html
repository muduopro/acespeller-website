Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-07

Welcome to AceSpeller. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data securely in accordance with the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile picture (if provided via Google or Apple Sign-In).
  • Practice & Learning Analytics: We record the results of your practice sessions, including scores, session duration, total questions attempted, number of mistakes, and language/subject preferences, to track your learning progress and generate statistics.
  • Gamification Data: We collect gamification statistics, including daily check-in streaks, the number of perfect practice sessions, and unlocked achievement badge records, to power the App's achievement and rewards system.
  • Friend & Social Data: If you use the Friend system, we collect and store the display names, usernames, profile photos, and email addresses of both parties involved in a friend request. When you choose to share a dictation session with a friend, the full content of that session (including recognised text) is transmitted to and stored in the recipient's account.
  • Voice/Audio Output Data: The App uses Microsoft speech services to synthesise spoken audio for dictation playback (text-to-speech). Text is sent to Microsoft's servers solely for audio generation and is not permanently stored. We do not record or store your voice.
  • Device Information & Tracking: We use third-party services like Google for service operation and stability monitoring. These services may collect unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, AAID), IP addresses, and other technical metadata to help us improve app stability and user experience.
  • Feedback & Support Data: If you submit in-app feedback or a bug report, we collect the content of your message, your device model, operating system version, App version, and optionally a contact email address you provide. This data is used solely for support and App improvement purposes.
  • Image Data: If you upload images (e.g., via camera or photo library) for text recognition, those images are securely stored on Google servers (located outside Hong Kong) and associated with your account records. You may remove them at any time by deleting the corresponding dictation record.
  • Advertising Data: To support the free tier of our service, the App may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. The advertising service may collect your advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android) for ad delivery and measurement purposes. You may opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you grant permission for push notifications, we will collect your device's push notification token to send service-related notifications (e.g., friend requests, system announcements).

2. PICS (Personal Information Collection Statement) & Purpose of Use

Your personal data is collected voluntarily but is required for core functionality. We use your data securely to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain AceSpeller's educational services efficiently.
  • Manage your account and facilitate cloud synchronisation across devices.
  • Personalise your learning experience based on your history and performance.
  • Implement gamification features to reward your learning efforts.
  • Facilitate social features, including the Friend system and sharing dictation resources.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your feedback.
  • Conduct aggregated (non-identifiable) analytics to understand usage trends and improve our services.
  • Serve non-personalised or personalised contextual advertisements (for free tier users) to support App operations.
  • Send important service updates, security alerts, and administrative notifications (if push notifications are enabled).

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable laws. Specific retention policies include:

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account remains active.
  • Dictation Records & User Data: Your dictation history, custom lists, statistics, uploaded images, and shared content stored in your account are retained as long as your account is active.
  • Friend Requests & Friend List: Friend requests are retained until accepted, rejected, or withdrawn. Friend list entries are retained until removed by you, or until 30 days after your account is deleted.
  • Shared Dictation Content: Shared content stored in a recipient's account is retained until the recipient deletes it, or until 30 days after the recipient's account is deleted.
  • Feedback & Support Data: Retained for up to 12 months for support tracking purposes, then deleted.
  • Voice Data: Voice output is generated ephemerally via Microsoft services and is not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Analytics & Crash Report Data: Processed in accordance with Google's default data retention settings (typically no longer than 14 months).
  • Account Deletion: If you request to delete your account via the "Delete Account" option in Settings, we will remove your personal identification data and all associated uploaded images from our active databases within 30 days, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (e.g., for legal disputes or tax audits).

4. Data Sharing and Cross-Border Transfers

  • Third-Party Providers: We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., Google, Microsoft) who assist us in operating our App, and require them to observe strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Cross-Border Transfer: You acknowledge and agree that your personal data may be transferred to, and stored on, servers located outside of Hong Kong, primarily at Google data centres (including locations in the United States, Taiwan, Singapore, and other regions) and Microsoft data centres (for speech synthesis). For such transfers, we rely on the standard contractual protections adopted by the relevant data centre providers (including Google LLC and Microsoft Corporation) to ensure that your data is protected to a standard no less than that required by the PDPO. You may review each provider's privacy policy for details on their data storage locations and safeguards.

4A. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised or accidental access, processing, erasure, loss, or use, including:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted via TLS (Transport Layer Security).
  • Access Controls: Access to personal data is subject to strict authentication and authorisation controls (provided by Google security rules).
  • Protection of Uploaded Images: Images you upload are accessible only to your own authenticated account.

Please note that while we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. In the event of a data security incident, we will notify affected users and relevant regulatory authorities in a timely manner in accordance with the guidelines of the Office of the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data (PCPD).

5. Your Rights (PDPO Compliance)

Under the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request ascertainment of whether we hold personal data relating to you, and request access to that data.
  • Correction: Require us to correct any personal data relating to you that is inaccurate.
  • Opt-out of Direct Marketing: We will not use your personal data for direct marketing without your prior explicit consent. If you have consented to receive any direct marketing communications, you may withdraw that consent in writing at any time, free of charge and without giving any reason.

How to Exercise Your Rights:
To submit a data access or correction request, please contact our Data Protection Officer in writing (including by email): cs@acespeller.com.hk

  • In accordance with Section 21 of the PDPO, we will respond to a valid request within 40 days of receipt.
  • We may charge a reasonable administration fee for data access requests as permitted by law.

6. Children's Privacy

AceSpeller offers educational tools suitable for various age groups. However, our account system requires users to have sufficient capacity to agree to our Terms.

  • If you are under 14 years of age (or the age of legal consent in your jurisdiction): You must obtain verifiable consent and involvement from a parent or legal guardian before creating an account or providing any personal data.

Special Safeguards for Children's Data:

  • We do not actively or knowingly collect personal data from children beyond what is necessary to provide the educational service.
  • Our advertising service will disable behavioural targeted advertising for identified child users.
  • If a parent or guardian believes that their child has created an account or provided personal data without authorisation, please contact us immediately at cs@acespeller.com.hk. We will delete the relevant data within 14 business days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new privacy practices.

隱私權政策

生效日期： 2026-03-07

歡迎使用 AceSpeller。我們致力於保護您的隱私，並依據《香港個人資料（私隱）條例》（PDPO）安全地處理您的資料。

1. 我們收集的資訊

  • 帳戶資訊： 電子郵件地址、顯示名稱及個人頭像（若透過 Google 或 Apple 登入提供）。
  • 練習及學習分析資料： 我們記錄您每次練習的結果，包括分數、練習時長、嘗試題目總數、錯誤次數及語言/科目偏好，以追蹤您的學習進度並生成統計數據。
  • 遊戲化資料： 我們收集遊戲化統計數據，包括每日打卡連續天數、完美練習次數及已解鎖成就徽章的記錄，以驅動本 App 的成就及獎勵系統。
  • 好友及社交資料： 若您使用好友功能，我們將收集並儲存好友申請雙方的顯示名稱、用戶名、個人頭像及電子郵件地址。當您選擇與好友分享某次默書練習時，該練習的完整內容（包括辨識文字）將被傳輸至並儲存於接收方的帳戶中。
  • 語音／音訊輸出資料： 本 App 使用 Microsoft（微軟）提供的語音服務將文字合成語音，用於默書播放（文字轉語音）。待朗讀的文字僅為生成音訊而發送至 Microsoft 伺服器，不會被永久儲存。我們不會錄製或儲存您的聲音。
  • 裝置資訊與追蹤： 我們使用 Google 等第三方服務進行服務運營及穩定性監控。這些服務可能會收集唯一的裝置識別碼（如 IDFA、AAID）、IP 地址及其他技術元數據，以協助我們改善 App 的穩定性與用戶體驗。
  • 意見反饋及技術支援資料： 若您提交 App 內意見反饋或錯誤報告，我們將收集您的反饋內容、裝置型號、作業系統版本、App 版本，以及您選填的聯絡電子郵件地址。此類資料僅用於技術支援及 App 改善目的。
  • 圖片資料： 若您透過拍照或相簿上傳圖片進行文字識別，該等圖片將被安全儲存於 Google 伺服器（位於香港境外），並與您的帳戶記錄相關聯。您可隨時通過刪除相關默書記錄來移除該等圖片。
  • 廣告資料： 為提供免費服務，我們的 App 可能顯示廣告。廣告服務（由 Google AdMob 提供）可能收集您的廣告識別碼（iOS 上的 IDFA 或 Android 上的 AAID）用於廣告投放及衡量。您可隨時在裝置設定中選擇退出廣告個人化追蹤。
  • 推播通知令牌： 若您選擇允許推播通知，我們將收集您裝置的推播通知令牌，以向您發送服務相關通知（如好友邀請、系統公告）。

2. PICS（收集個人資料聲明）及使用目的

您的個人資料乃自願提供，但是本應用程式提供核心功能所必需。我們將安全地使用您的資料以：

  • 有效提供、運營及維護 AceSpeller 的教育服務。
  • 管理您的帳戶並促進跨裝置的雲端同步。
  • 依據您的歷史記錄及表現提供個人化的學習體驗。
  • 實施遊戲化功能以獎勵您的學習成果。
  • 提供社交功能，包括好友系統及分享默書資源。
  • 提供客戶支援並回應您的意見反饋。
  • 進行匯總（不可識別身分）分析，了解使用趨勢以改善我們的服務。
  • 顯示非個人化或個人化的情境廣告（針對免費方案用戶），以維持 App 營運。
  • 發送重要的服務更新、安全警報及行政通知（若已啟用推播通知）。

3. 資料保留與刪除

我們只在達成本政策所列目的所需期間，或適用法律規定期間內保留您的個人資料。具體保留政策包括：

  • 帳戶資料： 只要您的帳戶保持活躍即予保留。
  • 默書記錄及用戶資料： 您的默書歷史、自訂列表、統計資料、上傳圖片及儲存於您帳戶中的分享內容，在帳戶活躍期間均會保留。
  • 好友申請及好友列表： 好友申請保留至被接受、拒絕或撤回為止。好友列表條目保留至您主動移除，或刪除帳戶後 30 天。
  • 已分享的默書內容： 儲存於接收方帳戶的分享內容，保留至接收方主動刪除，或接收方帳戶刪除後 30 天。
  • 意見反饋及技術支援資料： 保留最多 12 個月，用於技術支援追蹤，之後予以刪除。
  • 語音資料： 語音輸出由 Microsoft 服務短暫生成，不會永久儲存於我們的伺服器。
  • 分析及崩潰報告資料： 按 Google 的默認數據記錄設定處理（通常不超過 14 個月）。
  • 帳戶刪除： 若您透過設定中的「刪除帳戶」選項要求刪除帳戶，我們將在 30 天內從活躍資料庫中移除您的個人識別資料及所有關聯的上傳圖片，除非法律另有規定（例如用於法律糾紛或稅務審計）。

4. 資料分享與跨境傳輸

  • 第三方服務提供商： 我們不會出售您的個人資料。我們可能會與協助我們營運 App 的受信任服務提供商（如 Google、Microsoft）分享資料，並要求其遵守嚴格的保密義務。
  • 跨境傳輸： 您承認並同意，您的個人資料可能會被轉移並儲存於香港以外的伺服器，主要位於 Google 的數據中心（包括美國、台灣、新加坡等地區）以及 Microsoft 的數據中心（用於語音合成）。在進行此類轉移時，我們依賴相關數據中心提供商（包括 Google LLC 及 Microsoft Corporation）所採用的標準合約保護措施，以確保資料受到不低於 PDPO 所要求之保護水準的保障。您可在各服務提供商的隱私政策中了解其數據儲存地點及保護措施。

4A. 資料安全

我們採取合理的技術及組織安全措施，以保護您的個人資料免遭未經授權或意外的存取、處理、刪除、遺失或使用。措施包括：

  • 傳輸加密： 所有在裝置與伺服器之間傳輸的資料均透過 TLS（傳輸層安全協議）加密。
  • 存取控制： 個人資料的存取受嚴格的身份驗證及授權控制（由 Google 安全規則提供）。
  • 上傳圖片的存取保護： 您上傳的圖片僅限您本人的已驗證帳戶存取。

請注意，雖然我們盡力保護您的資料，但沒有任何通過互聯網傳輸或電子儲存的方法是完全安全的。若發生資料安全事故，我們將按照個人資料私隱專員公署（PCPD）的指引，及時通知受影響的用戶及相關監管機構。

5. 您的權利（符合 PDPO）

根據《香港個人資料（私隱）條例》（PDPO），您有權：

  • 查閱： 要求確認我們是否持有您的個人資料，並要求查閱該等資料。
  • 更正： 要求我們更正與您相關的任何不準確的個人資料。
  • 選擇退出直接營銷： 在未獲您事先明確同意的情況下，我們不會將您的個人資料用於直接營銷。如您已同意接收任何直接營銷訊息，您可隨時書面撤回同意，無需任何費用，且無需提供理由。

如何行使您的權利：
如欲提出資料查閱或更正要求，請以書面（包括電子郵件）方式聯繫我們的資料保障主任：cs@acespeller.com.hk

  • 根據 PDPO 第 21 條，我們將在收到有效要求後 40 天內回覆。
  • 我們可能依法收取合理的資料查閱行政費用。

6. 兒童隱私

AceSpeller 提供適合各年齡層的教育工具。然而，我們的帳戶系統要求用戶具備同意本條款的行為能力。

  • 如果您未滿 14 歲（或當地法定同意年齡）：在創建帳戶或提供任何個人資料前，您必須取得父母或法定監護人的可驗證同意與參與。

兒童資料的特別保護措施：

  • 我們不會主動或刻意向兒童收集超出提供教育服務所需的個人資料。
  • 我們的廣告服務將針對已識別的兒童用戶停用行為定向廣告。
  • 若家長或監護人發現其子女在未獲授權的情況下創建了帳戶或提供了個人資料，請立即聯繫我們（cs@acespeller.com.hk），我們將在 14 個工作天內刪除相關資料。

7. 政策變更

我們可能不時更新本隱私權政策。如有重大變更，我們將於 App 內發佈新政策以通知您。如您在變更後繼續使用本 App，即代表您接受新的隱私實務。
